About GenshinCo.de

Your source for active Genshin Impact redeem codes

GenshinCo.de is a fan-made website dedicated to tracking and sharing the latest active redeem codes for Genshin Impact. Our goal is to give players a fast, clean, and easy way to find codes and redeem them directly — no login required to browse.

What We Do

We curate active Genshin Impact codes from official sources such as HoYoverse live streams, social media announcements, and in-game events. Each code is listed with its known rewards so you can quickly see what you'll receive before redeeming.

Our "Redeem" button opens the official HoYoverse gift page with the code pre-filled, so you can log in and confirm the redemption directly on HoYoverse's secure platform. We never handle your account credentials.
