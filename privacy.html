Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: this site doesn't collect anything from you directly. The third-party services running on it do, and here's what that means.

Google Analytics

I use Google Analytics to see how many people visit and which pages they land on. It's basic traffic data, nothing tied to who you are. If you'd rather opt out entirely, the Google Analytics browser add-on does the job.

Google AdSense

There are ads on the site, served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you ads based on what you've been browsing elsewhere. You can manage that at Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

I don't set any cookies myself. The ones you'll see are from Google (Analytics and AdSense). You can block or clear them through your browser settings anytime.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit the site, a consent banner pops up asking whether you're okay with cookies. You can accept or reject -it's your call. If you reject, no tracking or ad cookies get set. Your choice gets saved in your browser's localStorage so we remember it next time you visit.

Want to change your mind later? You can clear localStorage for this site through your browser settings, or just click the Reset cookie preferences link. That'll wipe your choice and bring the banner back.

External links

The site links out to HoYoverse, Reddit, Game8, and a few others. Once you leave, their policies apply, not mine.

Changes

If something here changes, I'll update the date at the top. Anything unclear? Head to the About page.
