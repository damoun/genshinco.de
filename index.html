What Are Genshin Impact Codes?

Genshin Impact codes are special alphanumeric codes provided by the game developers, HoYoverse, as a way to reward players with exclusive in-game items. These rewards often include Primogems, Mora, Enhancement Ores, and other valuable resources that can enhance your gameplay experience.

How to Redeem Genshin Impact Codes

  1. Click the "Redeem" button on any code above — it opens the official HoYoverse gift page with the code pre-filled.
  2. Log in to your Genshin Impact account if prompted.
  3. Select your server and character nickname, then confirm the redemption.
  4. Check your in-game mailbox for the rewards!

Why Use Reward Codes?

Redeeming codes is a quick and easy way to get free resources without spending money. Codes are often released during live streams, special events, or game updates.
