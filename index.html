What are Genshin Impact redeem codes?

Genshin Impact redeem codes are alphanumeric strings released by HoYoverse, the game's developer. Each code grants free in-game rewards such as Primogems, Mora, Hero's Wit, or Enhancement Ores. Codes are typically distributed during livestreams, patch launches, and community milestones. They expire quickly, often within 12 to 48 hours of release.

How to redeem a code

The fastest way is to click "Redeem" on any code above, which pre-fills the code on the HoYoverse gift page. Just log in, select your server and character, and confirm. You can also redeem codes directly in-game through the Settings menu. For detailed step-by-step instructions for both methods, see our full redemption guide.

Where do codes come from?

HoYoverse releases codes through several channels. The most reliable source is the version livestream, held roughly every six weeks before a major patch. Each stream typically reveals three codes that expire within 12 to 24 hours. Beyond livestreams, codes appear during in-game events, anniversary celebrations, collaboration promotions, and social media milestones. HoYoverse occasionally distributes compensation codes via in-game mail when server issues or bugs affect players. Some promotional codes are region-specific, but most work globally. For a detailed breakdown, see our Code History and Patterns page.

Types of rewards you can get

The most sought-after reward is Primogems, the premium currency used for Wishes (the gacha system). 160 Primogems equals one Wish, and codes typically grant 60 to 100 Primogems each. Other common rewards include Mora (the universal in-game currency used for leveling characters, talents, and weapons), Hero's Wit and Adventurer's Experience (character EXP materials), Mystic Enhancement Ore (weapon EXP material), and consumable food items that restore HP or boost stats. During special events, codes may also grant exclusive namecard cosmetics, furniture for the Serenitea Pot housing system, or limited-time event currency. Learn more about every reward type in our Rewards Guide.

How often are new codes released?

New codes follow a predictable rhythm tied to Genshin Impact's six-week patch cycle. Each version livestream drops three codes, and occasional event codes appear between patches. Anniversary periods (late September) and Lantern Rite (Chinese New Year) tend to be especially generous. A handful of permanent codes exist — GENSHINGIFT is the most well-known — but most expire quickly. On average, expect three to six new codes per month.
