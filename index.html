What are Genshin Impact redeem codes?

Genshin Impact redeem codes are alphanumeric strings released by HoYoverse, the game's developer. Each code grants free in-game rewards such as Primogems, Mora, Hero's Wit, or Enhancement Ores. Codes are typically distributed during livestreams, patch launches, and community milestones. They expire quickly, often within 12 to 48 hours of release.

How to redeem a Genshin Impact code

  1. Go to the HoYoverse gift page, or click "Redeem" on any code above. It pre-fills the code automatically.
  2. Log in to your HoYoverse account.
  3. Select your server and character name.
  4. Click Redeem. Rewards arrive in your in-game mailbox within one minute.
